## AlphaSaaS - App Discovery

The **App Discovery** feature of AlphaSaaS helps businesses gain control over their SaaS applications by scanning and identifying all software in use. It uncovers subscriptions that may be underutilized, redundant, or unnecessary, allowing for optimization of SaaS spend. By offering detailed insights into usage and adoption across the organization, businesses can streamline their software stack, cut costs, and ensure that they are paying only for what is needed to meet their goals.

For more details, visit [AlphaSaaS](https://alphasaas.io).
